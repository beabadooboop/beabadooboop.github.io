Cauliflower Nuggets

Ingredients

  • 440g cauliflower florets
  • 20g mayonnaise
  • 1 courgette
  • 180g breadcrumbs
  • 400g cannellini beans
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoon fajita spice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic granules
  • 75ml boiled water

Instructions

  1. Add the cauliflower to a bowl and coat with the mayonnaise, breadcrumbs and half of the fajita spice. Roast for 20-25 mins.
  2. Chop the courgette into small chunks and pan-fry for 3 mins. Stir in the garlic and the other half of the fajita spice for 1 minute, then add the chopped tomatoes and boiled water. Bring to a simmer and leave for 5 mins.
  3. Drain and rinse the cannellini beans. Transfer a third to a bowl and crush with a fork. Add both the whole and crushed beans to the stew and cook for another 5 mins until thickened.
  4. Serve, topped with the cauliflower nuggets.

Morrocan Chicken

Ingredients

  • 450g diced chicken
  • 200g fine green beans
  • 30g diced onion
  • 120g cous cous
  • 30g chopped apricots
  • 1 teaspoon ras el hanout
  • 1 teaspoon garlic granules
  • 1 teaspoon flour
  • 1 chicken stock cube in 300ml boiled water for sauce
  • 1 chicken stock cube in 200ml boiled water for cous cous

Instructions

  1. Pan-fry the diced onion, ras el hanout and garlic for 5-6 min or until the onion has caramelised.
  2. Add the flour to the pan and cook for 1 min, then pour in the chiken stock water (300ml) and cook for 2-3 min until the sauce thickens.
  3. Once thickened, add the diced chicken, halved green beans and diced apricots to the pan. Reduce heat and cook, covered, for 12-15 min.
  4. While the chicken is cooking, pour the chicken stock water (200ml) over the cous-cous in a heat proof bowl then set aside. Fluff before serving.

Mushroom Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 250g Chestnut Mushrooms
  • 300g Baby Plum Tomatoes
  • 80g Spinach
  • 120g Spaghetti
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic granules
  • 1 teaspoon parsley
  • 1 teaspoon oregano

Instructions

  1. Add the spaghetti to a pan of boiling water and cook for 10 minutes, then drain.
  2. Slice the mushrooms and fry for 2-3 mins until softened.
  3. Add the tomato paste, plum tomatoes, garlic, parsley and oregano and cook for 4-5 mins until the tomatoes break down.
  4. Add the spaghetti to the pan and mix everything together.

Pan-Fried Salmon

Ingredients

  • 6x potato croquettes
  • 125g asparagus tips
  • 240g salmon fillets
  • 1 teaspoon dill
  • 1 teaspoon parsley

Instructions

  1. Add the croquettes to a lined baking tray and cook in the oven for 25-30 minutes.
  2. Put the asparagus on a roasting tray, drizzle with oil and season with salt and pepper. Roast for 12 mins until tender.
  3. Season the salmon fillets with the dill and parsley then pan-fry, skin-side-down, for 5 mins. Flip them over, lower the heat and cook for another 4 mins.

Prawn Noodles

Ingredients

  • 165g raw king prawns
  • 250g pak choi
  • 80g watercress
  • 300g udon noodles
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes in 900ml boiled water

Instructions

  1. Add the stock water, oyster sauce, soy sauce and udon noodles to a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Add the pak choi and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the prawns and watercress during the last 2 mins of the cooking process.

Scallop Linguine

Ingredients

  • 190g scallops
  • 300g cherry tomatoes
  • 80g spinach
  • 120g linguine
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic granules

Instructions

  1. Add the linguine to a pan of boiling water, cook for 6 mins then drain.
  2. Season scallops with salt and pepper, then add to a pan with a drizzle of olive oil, cooking for about 4 mins on each side. Transfer on a plate, cover to retain heat and set aside.
  3. Wipe pan clean and reheat, adding the tomato paste, cherry tomatoes and garlic granules. Cook for 4-5 mins until the tomatoes break down.
  4. Add the spinach and continue to cook until wilted.
  5. Add the linguine to the pan and mix everything together.
  6. Serve topped with the seared scallops.

Seafood Farfalle

Ingredients

  • 350g fish pie mix
  • 30g italian hard cheese
  • 30g rocket
  • 150g farfalle
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic granules
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 vegetable stock cube in 300ml boiled water

Instructions

  1. Mix the boiled water, vegetable stock, chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, garlic granules and chilli flakes together in pan.
  2. Add the sugar and stir through the farfalle, bringing to a boil.
  3. Transfer to casserole dish and put in the oven for 20 min.
  4. After 20 mins, remove from oven and add the fish pie mix. Return to the oven for a further 5 mins.
  5. To serve, stir through the grated Italian hard cheese and garnish with the rocket.

Seabass Salad

Ingredients

  • 2x seabass fillets
  • 2x little gem lettuce
  • 170g chargrilled artichokes
  • 120g cous cous
  • 1 teaspoon dill
  • 1 teaspoon parsley
  • 1 vegetable stock cube in 200ml boiled water

Instructions

  1. Pour the vegetable stock water over the cous cous in a heat proof bowl then set aside.
  2. Season the seabass fillets with the dill and parsley then pan-fry, skin-side-down, for 5 mins. Flip them over, lower the heat and cook for another 4 mins.
  3. Mix the artichoke into the cous cous, and then serve the cous cous on top of the lettuce leaves. Place the fish on top.
