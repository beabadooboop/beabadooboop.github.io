Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat a splash of olive oil and cook the garlic until golden. Add the pancetta and cook until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and quickly mix it with the pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat.
  5. Add the egg and cheese mixture to the pasta, stirring rapidly to create a creamy sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese if desired.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in the curry powder and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the coconut milk and bring to a simmer. Cook until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 200g mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 300ml beef broth
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the beef into strips and season with salt and pepper. In a pan, melt the butter and brown the beef on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, cook the onions and garlic until softened. Add the mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture.
  3. Stir in the flour and cook for another minute. Gradually add the beef broth, stirring constantly.
  4. Return the beef to the pan and simmer until the beef is tender.
  5. Stir in the sour cream and cook until heated through. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve over egg noodles or rice.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 broccoli head
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant. Add the vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  3. Stir in the soy sauce and hoisin sauce, and cook for another minute.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with rice or noodles.

Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Maple syrup for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
  6. Serve warm with maple syrup.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiling water until the batter is well combined (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on a wire rack. Frost as desired.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce. Place it in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add the Caesar dressing and toss until the lettuce is evenly coated.
  3. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the salad.
  4. Add croutons and toss lightly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately.

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cans (14.5 oz each) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, and dried basil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, carefully transfer the soup in batches to a blender and blend until smooth.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream if using, and heat through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot, with a side of crusty bread if desired.
